About Me

My name is Dr. Udaybhanu Singh Chandrawat , and I am honored to serve as the Professor and Head – Electronics and Communication Engineering and Additional Charge of Dean Student affairs and Research at Acropolis Institute of Technology & Research in Indore. In my role, I lead a team of educators who collaborate on designing and implementing innovative curricula and research projects in the domains of Electronics and Communication Engineering. One of the most rewarding aspects of my position is providing guidance and mentorship to students pursuing these fields, equipping them with the skills to thrive as accomplished professionals. Leveraging my extensive experience in academia and my passion for teaching and research, I have made significant contributions to the growth and success of my department and institution. Under my leadership a team of students and faculty members have developed a humanoid robot for Indore Airport to assist the passengers this is first such kind of humanoid robot installed at any airport in India. My team also has developed an traffic robot for Indore traffic police installed at Sikka school circle. I worked as Principal at Swami Vivekanand College of Engineering, Indore. I got an opportunity to work at SGSITS Indore under government of India’s reputed SMDP project. My research work published in reputed U.K. based SCI listed journal taylor and Francis.
